**Herb Roasted Chicken with Balsamic Reduction**

**Ingredients:**
- 1 whole chicken (about 4-5 lbs)
- 2 onions, peeled and chopped
- 4 potatoes, peeled and chopped
- 4 carrots, peeled and chopped
- 3-4 cloves garlic
- Fresh rosemary sprigs
- Salt and pepper to taste
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 1/4 cup balsamic vinegar
- 1/4 cup red wine
- Freshly chopped parsley (optional)
- 1 lemon (optional)

**Tools:**
- 9x13 inch roasting pan
- Aluminum foil
- Paper towels
- Basting brush
- Meat thermometer

**Instructions:**

1. **Preparation:**
    - Gather all ingredients and tools. Ensure all utensils and cooking equipment are clean and available.
    - Preheat the oven to 425°F (220°C).

2. **Prepare the Chicken:**
    - Remove the neck and giblets from the chicken. Rinse the chicken inside and out, then pat it dry with paper towels.
    - Season the cavity and outside of the chicken with salt and pepper. Insert garlic cloves and rosemary sprigs inside the cavity.

3. **Prepare the Vegetables:**
    - Peel and chop the onions, potatoes, and carrots.
    - Line the bottom of the 9x13 inch roasting pan with aluminum foil.

4. **Assemble in Baking Dish:**
    - Arrange half of the chopped onions on the bottom of the roasting pan.
    - Place the seasoned chicken on top of the onions.
    - Arrange the chopped potatoes and carrots around the chicken in the pan.

5. **Season and Start Cooking:**
    - Pour the balsamic vinegar and red wine over the chicken and vegetables, then drizzle with olive oil.
    - Cover the pan with foil and place it in the oven. Bake for 40-45 minutes.

6. **Basting and Roasting:**
    - Remove the foil and baste the chicken. Reduce the oven temperature to 350°F (175°C). Continue roasting.
    - Baste the chicken every half hour. Roast until the internal temperature of the chicken reaches 165°F (74°C) and the vegetables are tender.

7. **Rest and Reduce Jus:**
    - Remove the chicken from the oven and tent with foil. Allow it to rest for 10-20 minutes.
    - Strain and skim the pan juices. Boil the juices to reduce until they reach a desirable consistency. Season to taste.

8. **Carving and Plating:**
    - Carve the rested chicken and arrange it on a serving platter.
    - Drizzle the balsamic reduction over the chicken and vegetables.

9. **Final Touches:**
    - Optionally, sprinkle freshly chopped parsley and squeeze lemon juice over the dish for garnish and added flavor.

10. **Presentation and Serving:**
    - Perform a final check on the presentation of the dish, making any necessary adjustments. Serve immediately.

11. **Clean Up:**
    - Clean up the cooking area and wash the used utensils and equipment.

Enjoy your delicious Herb Roasted Chicken with Balsamic Reduction!
